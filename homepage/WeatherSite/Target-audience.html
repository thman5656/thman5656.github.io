description of the target audience

The target audince will be members of the community at large, that have a desire to know what is going on in their community. they could want to know about anything from what the weather is to what evenets are going on in town, to the crime rates. this site should be able to give them the information that they want.

two personas

Persona 1

Persona one will be a man that is interested in what the weather for today is so that he can know what time of the day would be best for golfing, and other outdoor activities hes retired and dosnt have any need to know whats going on in the future. He only cares about whats going on that day

Persona 2

Persona 2 is a young mother of 3 kids who needs to know what the weather is going to be like every day of the week so she can get her kids ready for what ever it might be. she is especially focused on what the weather will be like on satruday and sunday, because those are the days that she will be doing most of her sporting outings with her kids.
